Pests & Diseases

  • Fall Armyworm - Affects maize; look for leaf damage.
  • Blight - Affects tomatoes; leaves turn brown.
  • Aphids - Affects beans; small insects on stem and leaves.

Planting Precautions

  • Ensure proper spacing between crops.
  • Use certified seeds for better yield.
  • Regularly inspect for pests and remove infected plants.
