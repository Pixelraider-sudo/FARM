☕ Coffee Farming Details

Your complete guide to coffee cultivation, pests, diseases, and market info.

1️⃣ Best Planting Time

  • Long rains: March – May
  • Short rains: September – November

2️⃣ Soil Requirements

  • Well-drained fertile soil
  • pH 6 – 6.5

3️⃣ Common Pests Affecting Coffee

  • Coffee berry borer
  • Aphids
  • Leaf miners

4️⃣ Common Diseases

  • Coffee leaf rust
  • Coffee wilt disease
  • Brown eye spot

5️⃣ Current Market Prices

Kenya: 1 kg = KES 400 – 600

International (USA): 1 lb ≈ $3.50

6️⃣ Marketing Agencies & Support

  • Kenya Coffee Producers Association
  • Agricultural Marketing Society of Kenya (AMSK)
  • National Cereals & Produce Board
